1. This Is How I Start

The hands play a large part in assessing a person's health and that's how I pretty much begin every session. I don't play with tuning forks, setup smoke signals or do a dance. I go straight to the source and find out what's going on with the body. I can usually tell what's going on. I've been called me a >hilot, reiki, etc. I've been doing this long enough to know that doctors may find the symptoms but I find the cause.

2. I May Use Some Oils

I usually work my way around from the hands and towards the arms. I typically use oils to help with the massage. This is typical for all my sessions. I use mostly a seed-based oil that's safe - which I've been using for decades. If you have an issue with oil smells let me know. Other than that, this is pretty much SOP in my book.

3. The Massage Itself

These aren't my hands but it does illustrate what you can expect. I don't line up stones on your back or play "chop-chop" with your shoulders. I don't have a fancy bed but I do have a perfectly functional and very clean and comfortable massage table with a face fitment. Throughout the treatment, you will feel me work on some pressure points - mostly to relieve them from stress or other ailments.

One Service. Three Steps. That's all.

Just healing and 3 things. I'm not doing anything fancy. I just tend to focus on the basics. I may not have your favorite soundtrack but I may accomodate you with your favorite Netflix show. In all cases, I'm here to help out and hopefully get your body and its energy in the place where it needs to be.
